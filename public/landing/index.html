Voice AI infrastructure you can deploy, operate, and trust.

VoxForge is a modular monolith for enterprise voice agents — one pipeline for WebSocket, programmatic onboarding, and LiveKit WebRTC, with evaluation, replay, knowledge retrieval, MCP tools, and human handoff built in.

Product overview

Everything a pilot customer or engineering team needs to go from clone to production voice agent.

Voice pipeline

STT → agent orchestrator → TTS with per-turn evaluation, tracing, and outcome decisions.

Human handoff

Yes. Ubuntu 24.04 + Docker. See the deployment guide.

Is this production-ready?
